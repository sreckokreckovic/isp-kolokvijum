##### AES-GCM ####
U mainu dogovorimo kljuc:
final Key key = KeyGenerator.getInstance("AES").generateKey();
//Alice
Prvo napisemo poruku:
final String text = "I hope you get this message intact"
Generisemo bajtove:
final byte[] pt = text.getBytes(StandardCharsets.UTF_8);
Biramo cipher koji koristimo:
final Cipher encryption = Cipher.getInstance("AES/GCM/NoPAdding");
encryption.init(Cipher.ENCRYPT_MODE,key);
final byte[] ct = encryption.doFinal(pt);
Saljemo bobu:
send("bob",encryption.getIV());
send("bob",ct);
//Bob
Primimo poruke:
final byte[] receivedIv = receive("alice");
final byte[] receivedCt = receive("alice");
Biramo cipher i dekriptujemo:
final Cipher decryption = Cipher.getInstance("AES/GCM/NoPadding");
decryption.init(Cipher.DECRYPT_MODE,key,new GCMParameterSpec(128,receivedIv));
Dobijemo rezultat:
final byte[] dec = decryption.doFinal(receivedCt);
##### RSA #####
Na pocetku generisemo parove RSA kljuceva u mainu:
final KeyPair aliceKP = KeyPairGenerator.getInstance("RSA").generateKeyPair();
final KeyPair bobKP = KeyPairGenerator.getInstance("RSA").generateKeyPair();
//Alice
Kreiramo poruke i uzimamo bajtove poruke:
final String text = "Hi,Bob.It's Alice";
final byte[] textBytes = text.getBytes();
Biramo cipher koji koristimo:
final Cipher cipher = Cipher.getInstance("RSA/ECB/OAEPPadding");
cipher.init(Cipher.ENCRYPT_MODE,bobKP.getPublic());
final byte[] encrypted = cipher.doFinal(textBytes);
Saljemo bobu poruku:
send("bob",encrypted);
//Bob
Prima poruku:
final byte[] receivedCt = receive("alice");
Bira cipher:
final Cipher cipher = Cipher.getInstance("RSA/ECB/OAEPPadding");
cipher.init(Cipher.DECRYPT_MODE,bobKP.getPrivate());
I dobijamo poruku koju smo dekriptovali:
final byte[] decrypted = cipher.doFinal(receivedCt);
